# Newsletter Strategy

**Date:** 2026-05-16  
**Status:** Planning Phase  
**Owner:** DOS Aros Growth  
**Goal:** Build recurring subscriber list + monetization

---

## Why Newsletter?

**Value for Users:**
- Perlas (actuaciones destacadas) = exclusive content
- Predictions (next week's big matchups)
- Analysis that doesn't fit Twitter
- Exclusive merchandise drops

**Value for DOS Aros:**
- Direct channel (don't rely on social algorithm)
- Email list = asset (can email 1000s for free)
- Monetization: affiliate links, sponsorships, merchandise
- Retention: weekly touchpoint = better engagement

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. Perla de la Semana
**Format:** 1 deep-dive on best individual performance  
**Example:** "LeBron's 30pt game vs Celtics: The 3 moments that won the series"  
**Length:** 2-3 paragraphs + 1 chart/stat table  
**Frequency:** 1× weekly (Tuesday)

### 2. Predicción semanal
**Format:** AI-generated prediction for next week's key game  
**Example:** "Warriors vs Lakers (Fri): Will Curry's 3pt % decide?"  
**Content:** Matchup preview + historical edge + prediction + "Watch this player" callout  
**Length:** 2-3 paragraphs  
**Frequency:** 2× weekly (Wed, Fri)

### 3. Noticias de ligas
**Format:** 2-3 brief updates from NBA/Euro/Liga ACB/FIBA  
**Example:** 
```
- Lakers clinch #1 seed (3-way tie tiebreaker)
- Barcelona wins EuroLeague (repeat)
- Spain's women's team qualified for Olympics
```
**Length:** 1-2 sentences each  
**Frequency:** As-needed (embedded in weekly)

### 4. Análisis táctico
**Format:** "Tactic of the week" — 1 coaching decision that mattered  
**Example:** "Why did Suns run pick-and-roll 67% in Game 4? (vs 41% Game 3)"  
**Length:** 3-4 paragraphs + 1 video/gif  
**Frequency:** Bi-weekly (alternate with prediction week)

### 5. Exclusivas de mercancía
**Format:** "New drop: DOS AROS x [Team] collection"  
**Content:** Product images + discount code (newsletter-only)  
**Frequency:** 1-2× monthly (based on inventory)

## Growth Strategy

### Signup Funnel

**Landing Page:**
- CTA: "Weekly Perlas + Predictions" (below fold)
- Form: Email only (no name required first time)
- UX: Inline form, minimal friction
- CTA button: "Suscribirse"

**Incentive (Welcome Gift):**
- "Get free predictions for this week's playoff games"
- Immediate email (24h): 3 AI-generated predictions
- Builds habit: comes back to see if prediction was right

### Conversion Rate Target
- Landing page conversion: 5% (50 emails per 1000 visitors)
- Retention (open rate): 35% (industry avg 21%)
- Engagement (click rate): 8% (industry avg 2-3%)

### Growth Levers
1. **Social proof:** "Join 5000+ basketball fans" (when reach it)
2. **FOMO:** "Only in our newsletter: playoff insider analysis"
3. **Exclusivity:** Merchandise drops first for subscribers
4. **Reciprocity:** Free predictions today → trust → paid (future)

## Monetization

### Channel 1: Affiliate Links
**Example:** "Best basketball analytics tools"
```
1. Statsbomb Pro — $199/mo (YOUR AFFILIATE LINK)
2. Synergy Sports — $500/mo
3. DOS AROS — Free + Pro tier
```
**Payout:** 10-20% commission per referral  
**Frequency:** 1× monthly (subtle)

### Channel 2: Sponsorships
**Example:** "This week's newsletter is brought to you by [Sportsbook/Betting App]"
- Start: $500-1000/month (when 5K subscribers)
- Scale: $2000-5000/month (when 20K subscribers)
- Vetting: Only brands aligned with brand (no predatory betting)

### Channel 3: Merchandise
**Direct:** Newsletter-exclusive discount codes
- "Subscribers get 15% off new DOS AROS x Lakers collab"
- Drives both revenue + click-through

### Channel 4: Premium Tier (Future)
**When:** 10K+ subscribers  
**Offer:** "DOS AROS Pro" — $9.99/mo
```
- Advanced predictions (model confidence scores)
```

## Anti-patterns to Avoid

❌ **Don't spam:** More than 2× weekly = unsubscribe flood  
❌ **Don't sell every email:** 80/20 rule (80% content, 20% sales)  
❌ **Don't clickbait:** "You won't believe this stat!" = lose trust  
❌ **Don't hard-push affiliate:** Mention only once per month  
❌ **Don't neglect mobile:** 60% open on mobile, test responsive design  
❌ **Don't forget from-name:** "DOS AROS (@dosaroscontact)" = personal touch
